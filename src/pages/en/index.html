One of the leading rental companies of Central Europe offering small and large utility vehicles

VIARENT is a member of the Delta Franchise Our company VIARENT Kft. was founded in 1994 (under the name VIA Bérautó). Since then it has become the market leader in Hungary.

Our industry has undergone several changes since the foundation of the company. Our partners: manufacturers and suppliers have undergone a major shift in their approach, and are now typically seeking to focus on their own production activities, which are not closely related to transportation.

As a consequence, transport by rented vehicles has come to the forefront as our offered constructions have several advantages over leasing. Carriers were quick to recognise the advantages of rental. The rapid changes in transport tasks may require rapid exchanges of equipment, which may seem impossible to accomplish with a 3-5 year leasing contract.

Viarent is a brand-free lessor of utility vehicles and is in contact with almost all of the major European manufacturers. Our fleet mainly consists of 3.5 ton vans, 6-18 ton trucks, semitrailers and tractors that are suitable for a variety transport tasks.

Retail business

In the view of great interest, we established our retail business in early 2020. Our short-term retail customers can also use modern, young, low-running vehicles (most of the fleet consists of vans, but of course other types of vehicles are also available).

We have developed a completely new tariff system, and thus we can provide a competitive service. Our credo: the success of our company lies in the diversity and high quality of the products and services we offer.

Our goal is to help our customers to be able to perform their transportation tasks as quickly, economically and efficiently as possible.
